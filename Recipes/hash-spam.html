Hashbrown Surprise

I just came up with this while making this website. It should slap. Please pair with The Liao Egg Supreme for bes results

Ingredients:

  • 2 Hashbrown patties
  • Kraft Single
  • Spam
  • (Optional) The Liao Egg Supreme

Instructions:

  1. Toast your browns in the oven (follow instructions on bag)
  2. Fry spam slice in a pan till crisp
  3. Fry egg, if you fancy
  4. once food is cooked, assemble like a sandwich. Enjoy!
