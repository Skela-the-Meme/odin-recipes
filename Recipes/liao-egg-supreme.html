The Liao Egg Supreme

The Liao Egg Supreme is the supreme way to fry an egg. Ask my buddy Eric, he knows how to fry an egg.

Here is what you will need:

  • 1 Egg
  • Oil
  • Your choice of spice

Thats it! Heres what you're going to do:

  1. pour enough oil to cover a small pan, heat on high
  2. when hot, break egg into pan. Let cook for 1 minute
  3. Add desired spices
  4. Baste the egg with the oil till cooked.

Voila! A Liao Egg Supreme! Enjoy with spam or hashbrowns!
