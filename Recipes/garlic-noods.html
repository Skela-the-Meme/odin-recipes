Garlic Noods

Garlic pasta is my absolute favorite easy food to eat. Here is what you need for some oily goodness

  • Lots of olive oily goodness
  • Handful of your favorite pasta
  • 6/7 cloves of garlic
  • Lemon (juice and zest)
  • Parmesan
  • (optional) bundle of cilantro

Heres what you're gonna do:

  1. Pour a lot of that oil into a ban or wok and bring it to a simmer
  2. Boil water. Mince garlic. Zest and juice lemon. Add garlic to oil
  3. cooka da pasta el dante(read box instructions)
  4. When garlic is golden brown, add some lemon zest
  5. Drain pasta, keep a cup of the water.
  6. Add pasta to pan/wok and stir around. Add lemon juice and zest. If you have it, add cilantro
  7. Serve with lots of grated parmesan, and enjoy!
